### **Crypto Pulse AI - Business Model Canvas (Draft)**

#### **Customer Segments**

-   **Primary Segment**: Active individual/retail cryptocurrency traders who are technically comfortable but feel overwhelmed by the volume of market information.
-   **Characteristics**: They actively manage their own portfolios, have experience with trading platforms, and are looking for a data-driven edge. This includes traders like yourself with experience in other markets (e.g., Forex) looking to apply sophisticated strategies to crypto.
-   **Estimated Size**: Millions of active crypto traders globally.
-   **Access**: Reach them via online trading communities (Reddit's r/CryptoCurrency, Discord/Telegram groups), crypto-focused social media influencers (on X/Twitter), and by publishing content (blog posts, analyses) on trading strategy websites.

#### **Value Propositions**

-   **Core Value**: To provide a decisive trading edge by transforming information overload and complex data into clear, timely, and actionable insights.
-   **Problems Solved**:
    1.  **Information Overload**: Aggregates and analyzes market data, news, and social media in one place.
    2.  **Signal vs. Noise**: Identifies genuinely market-moving events from background chatter.
    3.  **Reduces Emotional Trading**: Provides objective, data-backed evidence for trading decisions.
-   **AI Advantage**: The AI is essential for processing vast, unstructured datasets (news, social media) in real-time and, crucially, for identifying complex, non-obvious correlations—like your brilliant idea of linking geopolitical events via flight radar data to market volatility.
-   **vs Alternatives**: While other tools offer charting (Technical Analysis) or news feeds (Fundamental Analysis), Crypto Pulse AI uniquely **integrates both with unconventional alternative data**, providing a more holistic and predictive view of the market.

#### **Revenue Streams**

-   **Primary Model**: For the capstone, the value is internal (learning, portfolio project). Commercially, a **SaaS subscription model** is a perfect fit.
-   **Rough Pricing**: A tiered approach:
    -   **Free/Basic Tier:** Basic market data and news aggregation.
    -   **Premium Tier ($20-$50/month):** Unlocks advanced AI insights, alternative data signals, and sentiment analysis.
-   **Timeline**: Recurring monthly or annual payments.

#### **Customer Relationships**

-   **Acquisition**: Attract initial users through content marketing (e.g., publishing interesting findings from the tool), direct outreach in trading communities, and word-of-mouth.
-   **Retention**: Keep users engaged by consistently delivering high-quality, profitable insights and regularly adding new, unique data sources and features.
-   **Support**: Initially, direct support via email and a community Discord/Telegram channel.

#### **Channels**

-   **Discovery**: Users will find you through crypto forums, social media (X/Twitter), search engines (via your content marketing), and partner newsletters.
-   **Access**: A web-based application/dashboard that is accessible from any desktop browser.

#### **Key Activities**

-   **Data Management**: Building and maintaining robust data pipelines to continuously ingest, process, and clean data from multiple APIs.
-   **AI/ML Development**: Designing, training, and fine-tuning the machine learning models that generate insights and signals.
-   **Platform Development**: Building and improving the user-facing dashboard to ensure it is intuitive and valuable.

#### **Key Resources**

-   **Data**: APIs for historical/real-time market prices (CoinGecko, CryptoDataDownload), news feeds, social media sentiment (X/Twitter API), and your unique alternative data sources.
-   **Technology**: **Groq API** (for fast LLM inference), Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn), cloud hosting (AWS/GCP), a time-series database, and **Jupyter Notebooks** for R&D.
-   **Human**: Your combined expertise is the key resource! **Data Science/ML skills** to build the engine and **Trading Domain Expertise** to guide the strategy.

#### **Key Partners**

-   **Data Partners**: The providers of your market and news data (e.g., CoinGecko, news API vendors).
-   **Tech Partners**: **Groq** (for the core AI model), your chosen cloud provider (e.g., Google Cloud, AWS).
-   **Distribution (Future)**: Could partner with trading influencers or educational platforms to reach a wider audience.

#### **Cost Structure**

-   **Development**: Primarily your time and effort for this capstone project.
-   **Operations**: Monthly recurring costs for API subscriptions (premium data feeds can be costly), cloud hosting (compute & database), and LLM API usage.
-   **Customer**: Initially minimal; could include small marketing/advertising spend later on.
-   **Monthly Estimate (Prototype Phase)**: Very low. Likely **under $100/month**, as many services have generous free tiers perfect for development and low-volume use.
