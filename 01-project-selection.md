# Activity 1: Project Selection & Team Formation (90 minutes)

## 🎯 Objective
Form effective teams around viable AI product concepts that can be developed in 3 weeks.

## 📝 Your Pre-Work Recap
Before starting, gather:
- Your identified project topics and ideas
- Datasets you've found (Kaggle or other sources)
- Your preferred teammates and skills you bring
- Technical capabilities and interests

## 🔄 Process (90 minutes total)

### Step 1: Project Pitches (30 minutes)
**Individual Presentations (2 minutes each)**

Present your project idea covering:
1. **Problem Statement**: What specific problem does this solve?
2. **Target Users**: Who would use this product?
3. **AI Approach**: What AI/ML method would you use?
4. **Data Source**: What dataset(s) have you identified?
5. **Unique Value**: Why is this better than existing solutions?

**Template for Pitch:**
```
Project: [Name]
Problem: [Specific problem in 1 sentence]
Users: [Who needs this solved]
AI Method: [ML/DL approach you'd use]
Data: [Dataset source and size]
Why This: [Key differentiator]
```

### Step 2: Project Clustering & Evaluation (30 minutes)

**Clustering Similar Ideas (15 minutes)**
- Group similar projects together
- Identify common themes and approaches
- Look for complementary ideas that could be combined

**Feasibility Assessment (15 minutes)**
Rate each project cluster on:
- **Technical Feasibility** (1-5): Can we build this with our skills?
- **Data Accessibility** (1-5): Can we get quality data quickly?
- **User Problem Clarity** (1-5): Is the problem specific and important?
- **3-Week Scope** (1-5): Can we create meaningful MVP in 3 weeks?

**Use This Evaluation Matrix:**
| Project | Technical | Data | Problem | Scope | Total |

| Idea A  |     ?     |  ?   |    ?    |   ?   |   ?   |
| Idea B  |     ?     |  ?   |    ?    |   ?   |   ?   |

### Step 3: Team Formation (30 minutes)

**Skills Inventory (10 minutes)**
Create a class skills matrix:
- Technical skills (Python, ML libraries, cloud platforms)
- Domain expertise (finance, healthcare, marketing, etc.)
- Product skills (user research, design, business analysis)
- Soft skills (communication, organization, problem-solving)

**Team Assembly (20 minutes)**
Form teams of 2-4 people ensuring:
- **Complementary Skills**: Mix of technical, domain, and product expertise
- **Shared Interest**: Genuine excitement about the chosen project
- **Collaboration Style**: Compatible working styles and commitment levels
- **Role Clarity**: Clear understanding of who will lead what areas

**Team Formation Checklist:**
- [ ] 2-4 team members selected
- [ ] Project selected and agreed upon
- [ ] Primary roles assigned (technical lead, product owner, researcher, etc.)
- [ ] Communication methods established (Slack, Discord, email)
- [ ] Meeting schedule agreed for intensive 3-week period
- [ ] Shared workspace set up (GitHub repo, Google Drive, etc.)

## 📋 Deliverables

**Individual:**
- Completed project pitch using template above

**Team:**
- **Team Composition**: Names, roles, contact information
- **Selected Project**: Final project description with rationale
- **Skills Matrix**: What each team member brings
- **Communication Plan**: How you'll collaborate over 3 weeks
