# AIPM Bootcamp - Day 1: AI Product Scoping (Draft Level)

## 🎯 Goal of This Repository

Welcome to Day 1 of Capstone! This repository contains all the materials you need to transform your AI product ideas into structured, viable business concepts with realistic 3-week development scope.

**By the end of today, you will have:**
- ✅ Formed effective teams with complementary skills
- ✅ Selected viable AI product concepts for 3-week development
- ✅ Completed draft Business Model Canvas covering all key business aspects
- ✅ Integrated AI ethics considerations for responsible development
- ✅ Defined realistic 3-week scope with clear success criteria

## 📋 Why Draft-Level Scoping?

**This Week Is Discovery**: Days 2-4 will involve market research, prototyping and user research that may change your direction. Better to create solid business foundation now, then detailed execution planning on Day 5 after you've learned from users and markets.

**Today's Focus**: Create structured foundation for development without over-planning before validation.

**Business Foundation First**: Using proven frameworks (Business Model Canvas + AI Ethics Canvas) to ensure you're building something commercially viable and ethically responsible.

## 📚 What This Week Covers

### This Week's Focus: Discovery & Validation Phase
- **Most critical phase** for AI product success - get this wrong and everything else fails
- **Your bootcamp scope**: Deep dive into discovery methodology using proven frameworks
- **Business foundation**: Today's business model work guides all discovery activities
- **Output**: Validated product concept ready for detailed 3-week development planning

### How Today Fits Into Full Product Management Lifecycle

**Four Key PM Phases:**
1. **Discovery & Validation** ← **This Week's Focus**
   - What to build, for whom, why it matters
2. **Development & Testing** ← **Your 3 Weeks After**
   - How to build it, does it work
3. **Launch & Growth** (Future Skills)
   - Getting users, scaling, iterating
4. **Optimization & Evolution** (Future Skills)
   - Long-term success, market adaptation

**Day 5 Detailed Planning**: After discovery, user research, and prototyping (Days 2-4), you'll create detailed execution plans with work breakdown, sprint planning, and risk management.

## 📋 Framework Overview

### Business Model Canvas (Activity 2)
**Industry standard framework** covering 9 key business areas:
- Customer Segments, Value Propositions, Revenue Streams
- Customer Relationships, Channels
- Key Activities, Key Resources, Key Partners, Cost Structure

### AI Ethics Canvas (Activity 3) 
**Responsible AI considerations** layered onto business model:
- Stakeholder impact assessment
- Key AI risk identification (bias, fairness, transparency, accountability)
- Trust-building and risk mitigation strategies

### 3-Week Scope Planning (Activity 4)
**Realistic development timeline** with:
- Technical feasibility assessment
- MVP feature prioritization (must-have vs nice-to-have)
- Weekly milestone planning
- Risk identification and contingency planning

## 🎯 Quality Standards

### Business Model Quality Check
- Would a stranger understand your business from the canvas?
- Is your value proposition compelling and specific?
- Does your AI advantage justify the technical complexity?
- Is your revenue model realistic for your market?

### Scope Realism Check
- Can you honestly build this in 3 weeks with your team's skills?
- Are your success criteria measurable and achievable?
- Have you excluded enough features to focus on core value?
- Do you have backup plans for major risks?

### Team Readiness Check
- Does everyone understand and support the direction?
- Are roles and responsibilities clear for the 3-week sprint?
- Do you have sustainable communication and collaboration systems?

Be mindful that you can reasses who does what, and how you do things as a team periodically (for example weekly).
