# Activity 3: AI Ethics Integration (45 minutes)

## 🎯 Objective
Review your business model for AI-specific risks and add responsible AI considerations - focused on key risks that could impact your business, not comprehensive ethical analysis.

For more indepth AI Ethics understanding take a look at [AI Ethics Canvas](https://www.trystangoetze.ca/aicanvas.html), which you can revisit during the project work.

## 🔍 AI Ethics Quick Assessment (45 minutes)

### Why AI Ethics Matter for Your Business
- **Risk Prevention**: Avoid costly failures and reputation damage
- **User Trust**: Build confidence in your AI solution
- **Competitive Advantage**: Responsible AI as differentiator
- **Future-Proofing**: Prepare for increasing AI regulation

### Step 2: AI Risk Areas Review (20 minutes)

**Review Your Business Model for These Key AI Risks:**

#### Data & Privacy Risks (5 minutes)
- **Data Source**: Is your data ethically obtained?
- **User Privacy**: How do you protect user information?
- **Bias in Data**: What biases might exist in your training data?

#### Fairness & Discrimination Risks (5 minutes)  
- **Equal Performance**: Does your AI work equally well for all user groups?
- **Access Equity**: Who gets to benefit from your AI?
- **Bias Amplification**: Could your AI make existing inequalities worse?

#### Transparency & Trust Risks (5 minutes)
- **User Understanding**: Can users understand how your AI works?
- **Decision Explanation**: Can you explain specific AI outputs?
- **Trust Building**: What do users need to trust your AI?

#### Accountability & Control Risks (5 minutes)
- **Human Oversight**: Where do humans stay in control?
- **Error Handling**: What happens when your AI is wrong?
- **User Agency**: Can users override or appeal AI decisions?
